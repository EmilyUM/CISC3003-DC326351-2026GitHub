Information Retrieval Project

Project Overview

This project was created as part of my Information Retrieval coursework. This project focuses on analyzing the Douban Movie Top 250 list, one of China's most authoritative and popular movie rating rankings. The list aggregates the highest-rated films voted on by millions of users, covering a wide range of genres and eras. Our goal is to use web crawling technology to collect this data and perform a quantitative analysis of the distribution of movie genres.


Technologies Used

  • Gini coefficient and entropy
  • Python
